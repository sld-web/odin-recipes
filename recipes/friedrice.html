Easy Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a popular dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with various ingredients like vegetables, protein (such as chicken, shrimp, or tofu), and seasonings like soy sauce and garlic. Its savory flavor and satisfying texture make it a versatile meal.

Often prepared with leftover rice, fried rice is quick to make and can be customized to suit different tastes. This dish is a beloved staple in many cuisines worldwide, perfect for casual meals or special occasions.

Ingrediants

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste

Steps

  1. Assemble ingredients.
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, t hen immediately drain in a colander
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
