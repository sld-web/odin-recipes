Indian Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry from the Indian subcontinent is a beloved dish featuring tender chicken stewed in a rich, tomato-based sauce. Seasoned with aromatic spices, including a key blend of curry powder made from coriander, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder, it offers a warm and inviting flavor.

The cooking process involves sautéing onions, garlic, and ginger, then adding tomatoes and spices to create a savory base. Simmered until the chicken is tender, this comforting curry pairs perfectly with rice or bread, making it a staple in many households.

Ingrediants

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • ½ teaspoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ¾ cup coconut milk
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion until lightly browned.
  2. Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar, and salt. Continue stirring for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper. Simmer 5 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
